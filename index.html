Biography

This is Jun ZHAO, an Assistant Professor in the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore. I am a key member of the National Digital Trust Centre (DTC) (which is also designated as the Artificial Intelligence Safety Institute Singapore (SG AISI) by the Singapore government), and the Strategic Centre for Research in Privacy-Preserving Technologies & Systems (SCRIPTS), led by Prof LAM Kwok-Yan, Prof WANG Huaxiong, and Prof CHI Chi Hung. SG AISI will collaborate internationally with (US/UK/Australia/Japan/China/...'s) AI Safety Institutes to advance the sciences of AI safety for national AI governance and international frameworks. I received a PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) in the USA, affiliated with CMU's renowned CyLab CyberSecurity & Privacy Institute (working on cybersecurity with Virgil Gligor, Adrian Perrig, and Osman Yagan), and a bachelor’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in China.

I am an Editor of many journals: IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (TIFS), IEEE Internet of Things Journal (IoTJ), ACM Distributed Ledger Technologies (DLT), and Elsevier Future Generation Computer Systems (FGCS). I have received best editor awards from IEEE journals and best paper awards from conferences/journals.
